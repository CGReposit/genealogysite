Historical Notes

Jeannette Angela Malcuit was born in 1926 to Maurice and Maria “Mary” Malcuit. Her father Maurice had emigrated from France, departing from the Port of Le Havre to begin a new life in America.

She married Edward Bradley Commerford, known as “Pali,” who was born in 1918 in Jersey City, New Jersey. Together they raised their family, and Jeannette lived a long life spanning nearly a century, witnessing tremendous changes in American society throughout the 20th and into the 21st century.

Jeannette passed away in 2020 at the age of 94, having lived through significant historical events including the Great Depression, World War II, the Civil Rights Movement, and the digital revolution. She was a devoted mother to Brad and helped maintain strong family connections across generations.
