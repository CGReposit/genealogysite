Historical Notes

Ellen Mae “Ella” Youmans was born in Jersey City, New Jersey, to George E. Youmans and Fanny O’Neill. She married John Rowe Commerford in the early 1910s, and together they built their life in Bergen County, New Jersey.

Ella devoted her life to raising her family through a time of immense change in America. She lived through two World Wars, the Great Depression, and the dawn of the modern era, seeing all five of her children grow into adulthood.

She passed away in 1973 in Westwood, New Jersey, and was laid to rest beside her husband at Pascack Cemetery in Park Ridge, Bergen County.
