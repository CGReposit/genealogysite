Historical Notes

Summary: Rachel Sliker was born in New Jersey in 1842 to Jacob and Margaret (Young) Sliker, members of a long-established Sussex and Morris County family of Dutch descent. She married Aaron W. Youmans around 1864, and together they raised at least one son, George E. Youmans.

Throughout her life, Rachel appears in multiple federal census records, consistently in rural northwestern New Jersey communities. Her family’s occupations were largely agricultural. She lived well into the early 20th century, appearing still alive in the 1910 census.
