Historical Notes

Maria Ferris was born in New Jersey in 1806 into a large Ferris family during the early decades of the United States. Her childhood coincided with a period of national consolidation following the American Revolution.

She married John Commerford and became the mother of Charles C. Commerford, linking the Ferris family to later generations prominent in American civic and sporting history.
