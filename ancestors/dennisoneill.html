Historical Notes

Summary: Dennis O’Neill was born in Ireland in 1840, during the height of the Great Famine. He emigrated to the United States as a young man and settled in New Jersey, where he married Mary Jane Ring.

He worked as a laborer and later as a farmhand in Bergen County, NJ, helping to establish one of the earliest Irish-American families in the Westwood area. Dennis died in 1906, leaving behind a family whose descendants remained in the region for generations.
