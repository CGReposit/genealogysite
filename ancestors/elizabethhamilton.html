Historical Notes

Summary: Elizabeth Hamilton, sometimes recorded as “Elizabeth Ann Ferris Hamilton,” was born 18 July 1859 to Alexander James Ferris Hamilton and Elize Charlier. She married Charles C. Commerford of New York, linking the Commerford and Hamilton lines. Their son John Rowe Commerford continued the family line through the 20th century.

Elizabeth was part of the Hamilton–Ferris branch that traces back to Alexander James Cranston Hamilton (b. 1788) and Mary Deane (b. 1759), both of Scottish and Irish descent. Census and church records place her family in New York City during the mid-19th century.
