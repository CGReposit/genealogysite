Historical Notes

Summary: Mary Jane Ring was born in Ireland in August 1832 and emigrated to the United States during the Great Famine period. She settled in New Jersey where she married Dennis O’Neill in the 1850s. Together they raised a large family in Bergen County.

She outlived her husband by two decades and was remembered as a pillar of her family and community. Her longevity and resilience make her one of the longest-lived known ancestors in the O’Neill line.
