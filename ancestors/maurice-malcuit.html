Historical Notes

Summary: Maurice Malcuit was born in 1897 in the Paris region of France. He emigrated to the United States as a child and lived the rest of his life in northern New Jersey. He married a woman who was also of immigrant origin and they had two children. He died in 1966.

Immigration: Arrived at the Port of New York on 26 January 1906 (entered with mother Maria Malcuit and brother). According to immigration records, they were to meet his father (listed as Jean) in New York. While the family's entry in 1906 is registered, a search of the database yeilded no results for his father.
